Car Prices vs Year with Brand & Price Filters

This is an interactive treemap where you can click on certain boxes to go more in depth into certain brands. Hovering over the boxes also tells us more information about how many cars within the dataset is that model or that brand.

Choosing a treemap to show a relationship between car brand and model will show how each model and brand participates in the data. The color grid shows that the darker the color, the more the car model is in the data. The car model that is the most in the data will be the Honda Accord, with 365 cars; the following will be a Ford Fiesta, with 363 vehicles. The model with the lowest count will be the Mercedes C-class 306 and the Honda CR-V 308 vehicles.
