Car Price By Fuel Type

Choosing a side-by-side box plot to compare the price allows us to see some essential statistics of the data, which contains the mean, maximum, minimum, and range for the price in each category. We can see an interesting situation: all fuel types have a similar minimum car price of 2000. Electric cars have the highest mean price of 10,000 for the four fuel types, and diesel cars have the lowest mean price of 8,000. The range for the min/max and the range inside the interquartile range is similar for electric and hybrid vehicles. The result is backed up by the idea that they have similar technology as hybrids, which means they use a combination of electricity and regular gasoline.
