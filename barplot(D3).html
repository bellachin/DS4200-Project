The visualization below presents a histogram showing the distribution of used car prices in the dataset. Each bar represents the number of cars that fall within a specific price range, allowing us to quickly identify pricing trends across the market. The tallest bars indicate price ranges where the majority of vehicles are concentrated, highlighting common affordability zones. This distribution helps consumers, sellers, and analysts understand the typical cost of a used car and can inform pricing strategies or purchasing decisions.
