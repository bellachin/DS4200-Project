Bar Chart of Car Prices(clickable)

The linked bar chart below the histogram provides deeper insight by showing the average price per brand within each selected price range. While the histogram reveals how many cars fall into each price bin, this chart helps identify which brands dominate or differ in pricing within that same range. This comparison allows viewers to spot brands that offer greater value or carry premium pricing even among similarly priced vehicles. It adds an extra layer of analysis for both consumers and market analysts exploring brand-level pricing trends.
