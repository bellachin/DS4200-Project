Car Prices vs Year with Brand & Price Filters

I chose an interactive scatter plot for this because it allows us to see the price distribution for each year easily and provides a tooltip with additional information like Brand, Model, Year, Price, and Mileage. A filter to select a car brand and a slide bar to choose the budget is also helpful for searching the car market. We can see that the most expensive car in this data set is a 2023 Audi A3 with the scatter plot; moreover, if we select Audi as the only filter, we can see that the price of this A3 is much higher in comparison with other models or cars in 2023.
